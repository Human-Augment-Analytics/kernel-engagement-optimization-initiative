# Identifying Relevant Contacts
### Connecting You with the Right HAAG Experts

To ensure your inquiry is handled efficiently, HAAG utilizes a structured facilitation system. Use this guide to determine who you should speak with.

---

### Key Roles in the Engagement Process

| Role | When to Contact Them |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Engagement Facilitator** | When you aren't sure which project fits your needs or you have a general inquiry about HAAG. |
| **Project Lead** | When you have a specific technical question about an active research project listed on our website. |
| **Research Manager** | For discussions regarding contracts, IP, or long-term strategic institutional partnerships. |
| **Faculty Advisor** | For academic inquiries, guest lecturing, or high-level theoretical discussions. |

### How to Find Contact Info
1.  **Project Pages:** Every research project page includes a "Points of Contact" section.
2.  **The HAAG Directory:** A searchable list of members, searchable by expertise and current workstream.
3.  **The General Inquiry Portal:** For those new to HAAG, our central contact form routes your message to the appropriate Engagement Facilitator within 48 hours.

> **Note:** All communication is logged in our CRM. This ensures that even if your primary contact changes, your collaboration history remains intact and the transition is seamless.
