# Initiating Collaboration Discussions
### Your Step-by-Step Guide to Partnering with HAAG

Starting a collaboration should be as seamless as the research itself. Follow these steps to move from an initial idea to an active partnership.

---

### Step 1: Prepare Your Inquiry
To help us match you with the right team, please have the following information ready:
* The specific HAAG project or research area of interest.
* Your organizational background or individual expertise.
* A brief description of your collaboration goals (e.g., "We want to test your algorithm on our dataset").

### Step 2: Choose Your Channel
We are **communication-agnostic**. You can reach out via:
* **Email:** Use the contact address found on the specific Project Page.
* **Website Form:** Fill out our "Initiate Collaboration" form for a structured intake.
* **Direct Outreach:** If you already have a relationship with a HAAG member, feel free to contact them directly.

### Step 3: The Facilitation Workflow
Once you reach out, here is what happens:
1.  **Initial Review:** An Engagement Facilitator reviews your request to ensure it aligns with our current research goals.
2.  **Introduction:** You will be connected via email (or your preferred channel) to the relevant Project Leads.
3.  **Discovery Call:** We schedule a brief discussion to define the scope, timeline, and mutual expectations.
4.  **CRM Integration:** Your project and contact details are logged in our CRM to ensure consistent support across semesters.

### Step 4: Formalizing the Engagement
Once both parties agree on the scope, we provide a **Collaboration Roadmap** that outlines the next steps, meeting cadences, and reporting procedures.
