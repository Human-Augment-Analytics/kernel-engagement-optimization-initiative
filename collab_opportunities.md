# Collaboration Opportunities at HAAG
### Pathways for Partnership and Growth

The HAAG Engagement Initiative is designed to turn research into impact. We offer several structured pathways for external stakeholders to engage with our work.

---

### Collaboration Tracks

#### 1. Industry Partnerships
Designed for corporate entities looking to apply HAAG research to real-world challenges. 
* **Focus:** Pilot programs, technology transfer, and bespoke problem-solving.
* **Benefit:** Access to cutting-edge research before it hits the mainstream market.

#### 2. Academic & Lab Collaborations
For fellow researchers and external labs seeking to pool resources or expertise.
* **Focus:** Joint publications, shared data sets, and cross-institutional grants.
* **Benefit:** Multi-disciplinary synergy and expanded research reach.

#### 3. Prospective Contributors
For independent researchers or specialists who wish to contribute to specific HAAG workstreams.
* **Focus:** Code contributions, peer review, and technical validation.

### Our Commitment to Clarity
For every project listed on our site, we explicitly define:
* **Open Roles:** Specific areas where we are currently seeking external input.
* **Resource Requirements:** What we provide (data, expertise) and what we ask of partners.
* **Expected Outcomes:** Clear milestones for the collaboration.
